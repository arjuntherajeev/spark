In case you were wondering.. Yes, we most definitely are more than JUST a website!



Located in Dubai Jumeirah's bOx park, we bring you our center, CHillSpace
ChillSpace is the Center of your Interconnected communication web where people interested in the subculture come to Socialize, learn and express the artistic side of hip hop.






ChillSpace is a three story building which has a :

  • classic American Diner with the ambiance of graffiti and hip hop,
  • rooms with walls placed in a grid like manner dedicated for the purpose of graffiti,
  • rooms where talks are held,
  • exhibition rooms,
  • ... and areas for socializing.




chillspace also offers an external skate park and a stage for shows, concerts and competitions.
